## ðŸ“‹ 7-ELEMENT PARAGRAPH MODEL

Every paragraph follows this structure:

**1. TOPIC SENTENCE**
   - Clear statement of paragraph focus
   - Contains main claim or concept

**2. CONTEXT/BACKGROUND**
   - Why this matters
   - Industry relevance
   - Historical context (if applicable)

**3. DETAILED EXPLANATION**
   - Technical specifics
   - How it works
   - Key components or processes

**4. SUPPORTING EVIDENCE**
   - Data from Tier-1/2 sources
   - Statistics, measurements, specifications
   - Inline citations (Author page#)

**5. REAL-WORLD APPLICATION**
   - Fleet/technician perspective
   - Practical implications
   - Maintenance considerations

**6. LIMITATIONS/CONSIDERATIONS**
   - Challenges or trade-offs
   - Variables affecting performance
   - Conditions or exceptions

**7. CONCLUSION/TRANSITION**
   - Summarize key takeaway
   - Connect to next paragraph
   - Reinforce importance
